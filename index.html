Think you know broccoli? Think again. I'm here to make broccolini the star of your plate because it deserves the spotlight. If you haven't tried it yet, you're seriously missing out. Broccolini takes much less prep (no cutting stems!) and perhaps tastier due to its natural sweetness than broccoli.

Whether you're looking to elevate your meals, impress your friends, or trick the kids into loving their greens? The Chinese broccoli, also known as gai-lan, kai lan or Chinese kale (芥蓝) is your secret weapon. If you're grilling it, steaming it, or even just tossing it into a salad, I've got all the recipes and tips you need to make broccolini your new go-to vegetable.

About me

Wing Broccolini

Hello everyone, my name is Wing Broccolini and I am passionate about learning different ways of preparing and enjoying broccolini. I've experimented with all kinds of broccolis in so many ways, and honestly, it's one of the most versatile veggies you'll find. From grilling, steaming, to salting and even including it in a salad, it has become a household vegetable in my kitchen.

This is because what makes me most excited about broccolini is how easy it is to work with in almost every meal. It is healthy, tasty and can really enhance the flavor of any meal. On this site, I am going to be sharing some basic recipes that will help you include broccolini in your daily meals and eat healthy no matter what your cooking skills are. You can even pretend to be a giant eating trees!

My website is called Simply Broccoli because I specialize in broccolini. It is just broccoli, sleeker-but with a hybrid twist from China: proof that even the humblest things have their unique story behind them.

I've spent a lot of time figuring out how to make healthy food actually taste good, so you can trust that every recipe is something I'd genuinely enjoy myself. If you are in search of a quick and easy dinner idea or want to come up with something special, these recipes will change the way you think about broccolini, turning it into something you'll actually crave..

Are you in the mood for some exciting meals? You've come to the right place! I'm excited to introduce you to these recipes and I'm sure you'll learn to love broccolini as much as I do. Let's get cooking!
