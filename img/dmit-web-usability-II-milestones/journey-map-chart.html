Key Journey Map Insights:

  1. Informal Progression: The current best-case user journey relies heavily on individual initiative and personal networking rather than structured pathways.
  2. Limited Recognition: Even highly engaged participants lack formal recognition as members.
  3. Communication Challenges: Reliance on Meetup.com and word-of-mouth limits effective communication about events and opportunities.
  4. Ad Hoc Engagement: Participation in special events happens through informal channels rather than a structured system.
  5. Knowledge Sharing Constraints: Limited mechanisms for structured skill sharing and learning despite interest from participants.
  6. Resource Limitations: Without membership fees, the club has limited resources for expanding offerings and improving experiences.
  7. Undefined Advancement: No clear path for advancement from casual attendee to club leadership or specialized roles.
  8. Missed Data Collection: Current journey fails to capture valuable information about members' interests, skill levels, and equipment that could inform better programming.
  9. No Revenue Stream: Without a formalized membership system, the club lacks a sustainable funding model for growth and enhancement.

How The Membership Application Will Help:

  1. Formalize the progression from casual attendee to recognized member
  2. Create structured communication channels for all club activities
  3. Establish clear benefits for membership to encourage deeper engagement
  4. Enable resource generation through membership fees to fund club activities and growth
  5. Collect meaningful data about members' skills, interests, and equipment
  6. Use member data to guide event planning and content development
  7. Facilitate connections between members with similar photography interests
  8. Generate sustainable revenue through standardized $40 annual membership fees
  9. Provide metrics for tracking club growth and member engagement
