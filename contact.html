Perfect Picnic Spot: Hawrelak Park

One of my favorite spots to go is Hawrelak Park in Edmonton. It's peaceful, surrounded by tall trees and a beautiful lake. There are plenty of open grassy areas to lay out a blanket, and it's great for people-watching or just relaxing with snacks and sunshine. To get there, head west on Groat Road, turn right onto Emily Murphy Park Road, then follow the signs into the park.

Broccolini Excellence: The Rise of Purple Broccolini

Let me tell you something that's going to change your broccolini game forever - purple broccolini exists! Yes, you read that right. Just when you thought broccolini couldn't get any more exciting, Food & Wine drops this bombshell that has me absolutely thrilled.

In their latest feature on this superstar vegetable, Food & Wine revealed something that had me doing a double-take: "Developed in 1993, Broccolini is grown in Arizona and California as it mounts in culinary popularity, though we've seen a purple variety from Balsam Farms in East Hampton, New York." Purple. Broccolini. Can you believe it? While many of us are just now discovering the wonders of regular broccolini, culinary innovators are already taking it to the next level with vibrant purple varieties!

This revelation perfectly illustrates what I've been saying all along - broccolini isn't just another vegetable; it's an evolving culinary superstar that chefs and growers continue to innovate with. The purple variety coming from Balsam Farms in East Hampton adds another dimension to our broccolini adventures. I'm already imagining the stunning presentation possibilities - the vibrant purple stems against a white plate would make any dinner guest stop and take notice before they even taste the exceptional flavor.

Purple broccolini variety from Balsam Farms

For those who haven't fully embraced broccolini yet (seriously, what are you waiting for?), Food & Wine offers a perfect reminder of what makes this hybrid so special: "Broccolini looks like a long, thin version of broccoli with tender florets rather than tight heads. It's milder than traditional broccoli but can typically be used in the same way." That milder flavor profile is exactly why it's so versatile - it takes on other flavors beautifully while maintaining its distinct character and that perfect tender-crisp texture that regular broccoli often misses.

The article goes on to share a staple preparation method that confirms what I've been preaching about broccolini's versatility: "This staple Broccolini recipe is delicious with a variety of other vegetables as well, such as cauliflower, snap peas, or green beans. Garlic and olive oil balance fat and spice, adding depth of flavor to any mild vegetable, while red pepper adds a kick and complexity." This simple preparation showcases exactly why broccolini deserves the spotlight - it plays well with others while still maintaining its star quality.

What especially caught my attention is how the article confirms my long-held belief about broccolini's ease of preparation. They note you can have "a satisfying Broccolini side dish in half an hour with only 10 minutes of work." This matches perfectly with what I've been telling you - broccolini gives you maximum flavor reward for minimal kitchen effort! No more tedious stem-peeling or chopping through tough stalks like with traditional broccoli. Broccolini is the answer for busy food lovers who refuse to compromise on flavor or nutrition.

The existence of purple broccolini opens up exciting possibilities for those of us who are already broccolini enthusiasts. I'm now on a mission to track down these purple varieties for my kitchen experiments. Just imagine the Instagram-worthy plates you could create combining both traditional and purple broccolini! Not only would you elevate the visual appeal of your dishes, but you'd also be showcasing your culinary knowledge by featuring this cutting-edge vegetable variety.

Want to dive deeper into the world of innovative broccolini varieties and perfect preparations? Check out the full Food & Wine article at: foodandwine.com/recipes/garlicky-broccolini

Trust me, once you start exploring these broccolini variations, you'll never look at your vegetable drawer the same way again!

About me

Wing Brook

Hello everyone, my name is Wing Brook and I am passionate about learning different ways of preparing and enjoying broccolini. I've experimented with all kinds of broccolis in so many ways, and honestly, it's one of the most versatile veggies you'll find. From grilling, steaming, to salting and even including it in a salad, it has become a household vegetable in my kitchen.

This is because what makes me most excited about broccolini is how easy it is to work with in almost every meal. It is healthy, tasty and can really enhance the flavor of any meal. On this site, I am going to be sharing some basic recipes that will help you include broccolini in your daily meals and eat healthy no matter what your cooking skills are. You can even pretend to be a giant eating trees!

If you are in search of a quick and easy dinner idea or want to come up with something special, these recipes will change the way you think about broccolini, turning it into something you'll actually crave.
